Pesto Pasta

Picture of Pesto Linguini with some shredded cheese

This is an easy to make pasta, requiring little ingredients and minimal prep and cook time. This recipe will create 8 servings and would go well with a variety of vegetables and a nice piece of bread.

This recipe can be made vegan, just remove the parmesan. Keep in mind that many parmesan brands contain rennet, an animal-based product. Instead of adding parmesan, you could top with crushed cashews.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons pesto
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • tablespoons grated parmesan (or cashews) cheese
  • 1 (16oz) package pasta
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until done. Drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a frying pan over medium low heat. Add pesto, onion, and salt and pepper. Cook about five minutes, or until onions are soft.
  3. In a large bowl, mix pesto mixture into pasta. Stir in grated cheese. Serve.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 225 calories; protein 7.8g; carbohydrates 32g; fat 7.2g; cholesterol 43.5mg; sodium 71.3mg.
